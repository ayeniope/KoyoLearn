Frequently Asked Questions

See some of the questions asked frequently

1. What is Koyo?

Koyo is an E-learning continuing education platform that offers admission seekers into local and international tertiary institutions with educational materials (mostly redirection to Youtube video tutorials and links) coupled with past questions and answers for different subjects with a mock exercise and exam preparation materials that will help students practice before their examinations.

2. How do I sign up?

To sign up for an account with Koyo is very easy. You can sign up with your Name or Facebook or Gmail account. Yuur details are safe with Koyo.

3. What is a me and you chat and how does it work?

M&U chat is a personal interaction between our expert tutors and you. It permits you to probe challenging questions and get satisfactory answers via Koyo platform . You will be assigned to one of our expert tutors who will provide answers to questions asked.

4. What subjects are covered on Koyo?

All subjects covering Science, Arts and Commercial will be accessible on Koyo, including computing and local and international languages.

5. Can my course be locked?

To ensure every student is actively engaged while taking their course, course locking will be implemented to freeze a course if inactivity is detected or students stop interacting with course materials for 2 months.

6. Can I cancel my subscription?

You are entitled to a refund/return request within 14 days. After the expiry of this period, any refund/return request will not be processed.
If you are still within the allowed refund/return request window, please contact our customer service department on WhatsApp +234 801 234 5678 or email help@koyo.com, and we will process this request immediately . Please allow five (5) business days for your money to be returned to you.

7. What is the Big Sister and Big Brother Program about?

The Big Sister and Big Brother Program is a mentorship program provided by Koyo to interested students who wish to build confidence, be encouraged and empowered in personal development. Students will be allowed access to materials and mentors knowledgeable in different fields to help them identify and achieve their academic goals. This program will help to identify and correct gaps in generic skills and knowledge.

8. How can I contact if I need help?

For any other support issues, please contact our customer service department on WhatsApp +234 801 234 5678 or email help@Koyo.com
